Secure by Scenario

Build your team and test your incident response at the same time

This bespoke workshop involves initial consultation with your organisation to build out a tailored and realistic scenario. In a moderated workshop we will then work with groups of your people to run through the scenario in stages, establishing responses and communication flows, and helping you to prepare for the worst from every angle. Previous sessions have not only provided excellent learning opportunities for participants, they have also opened eyes to new perspectives through our team-based, moderated approach.

A package of customised injects and briefings mean that after the initial run, your teams can re-run the scenario from different angles and with different decisions to safely explore consequences.
