Advanced Threat Modelling

A comprehensive delve into threat modelling for your organisation

If you have now seen the benefits of threat modelling and decided the introductory workshop isn’t enough, our advanced course is a four day course for up to ten delegates. Delegates will delve into the fundamentals of the different approaches and how to use them effectively to ensure security by design.

The most common methods will be covered in multiple exercises designed to call out their component techniques and illustrate how to recombine them into a model designed for your specific business needs. For an added bonus, exercises can be carried out against your existing or planned systems.
