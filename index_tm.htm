TriMedia Classes

TriMedi processors are extremely powerful and flexible. This means they are very versatile, but their versatility also makes them complex to use. It is important to understand this complexity in order to get the best performance. It is important not to make poor design decisions at the begining of a project that will cause problems later on.

Chris has worked with TriMedia for many years now. He has excellent relations with the system and software architects in NXP and is in contact with them regularly as they develop new chips. Learning from them he develops classes to teach about the new chips.

After many years working in this way, he is in the unique position of having a good understanding of all the processors and software architectures. The understanding and insights that he can give you in a training class could help ensure good design choices resulting in a successful, on-time project.

TriMedia training classes:

  • TriMedia TSSA1: learn to build systems from components, write new components and work effectively with TriMedia layers
  • TriMedia CPU Core: learn to use the TriMedia core most effectively and use the tools to enable you to write more efficient programs
  • TriMedia Cache architecture: learn how the cache works so that you can work with it efficiently and avoid common pitfalls
  • TriMedia Optimization: learn strategies to guide your optimizations to develop efficient programs
  • TriMedia for Managers: be well-informed so you can make sensible decisions and recommendations during project planning
  • TriMedia for Sales People and FAEs: understand TriMedia so you can match your customer requirements to TriMedia solutions
