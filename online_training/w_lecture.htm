Lectures

Our on-line lectures come in two forms: with accompanying audio narration, and with written lecture notes for revision.

The diagrams for our on-line lectures are not just PowerPoint® slides of bullet points.

Our main slides are rich diagrams that are designed to be fully explained by an expert presenter. An audio narration allows you to study the diagram while it is being explained to you.

An overview of 'bullet points' is provided as an aid for revision. They are not used as prompts for the presenter to read out.

The bullet points are helpful to people whose first language is not English, because they give a written summary that can help in understanding the spoken English.
