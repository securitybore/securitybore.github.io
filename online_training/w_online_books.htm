Online books

The most familiar element of our on-line training is an on-line book.

Ours are well illustrated, dealing with each topic in depth but clearly explained and arranged so you can read as much or as little as you need at the time.

The 'page' format and informative navigation makes it easy to dip in to a topic that is of immediate interest, or to read sequentially to better understand the context.

Our approach is very much based on diagrams and listings, with explanations that aim to help you gain an intuitive understanding as well as a practical understanding of the issues that typically matter most to practising engineers.

Our on-line books are (depending on the topic) also supported by glossaries explaining key terms.
