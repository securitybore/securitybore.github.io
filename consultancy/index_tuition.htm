1-to-1 tuition in DSP and Image Processing

If you need to learn DSP but can't make it to one of our scheduled classes, then consider 1-to-1 tuition. Follow a rational, well organized program of learning and experiment but at dates and times that suit you.

The aim of 1-to-1 tuition is to let you learn DSP in a practical way through discussion, demonstration and experiment. We have a comfortable seminar room and two well-equipped DSP workstations to support this.

Tuition is based on our standard DSP classes, so we follow a well-planned learning program. I won't go beyond my range of teaching expertise but we can adapt the focus, topics and applications to your own experience and interests. Topics can include:

  • 1-to-1 DSP tuition
    • Characterizing signals and noise
    • Figures of Merit - estimating signal quality
    • Estimating appropriate design specifications
    • Frequency domain analysis
    • Discrete Fourier Transforms
    • Sampling, aliasing and resolution
    • Digital filtering
    • FIR digital filter design
    • Estimating filter length
    • Arithmetic precision and fixed-point implementations
    • Estimating and reducing computational complexity
    • FIR and IIR filter design
    • Image processing for consumer electronics
    • Compression of still and moving pictures
    • Video mixing, alpha blending and color keying
