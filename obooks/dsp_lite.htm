DSP Lite online book

This online book explains the fundamental issues of DSP including:

  • sampled data systems
  • Antialiasing and reconstruction
  • Convolution and correlation
  • Spectral analysis and the Fourier Transform
  • Purpose and use of FFT window functions
  • Digital filtering
  • FIR and IIR filters

The contents of this book are taken from our highly respected DSP course material to give a basic introduction to DSP. The book takes a practical approach and does not assume a lot of prior knowledge. If you want or need a more indepth approach then please look at our DSP Foundation online book.
