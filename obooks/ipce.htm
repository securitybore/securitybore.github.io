Images

The quality of images is a key factor for consumer products. Perceived image quality depends on a lot of factors, many based on the psychology of seeing but implemented in highly technical ways. In this book we explain how images and video are captured, processed, compressed, decompressed and finally rendered for the viewer in consumer electronics applications. We take a practical approach that clarifies the background and purpose of the technical processes as well as explaining them in depth.
