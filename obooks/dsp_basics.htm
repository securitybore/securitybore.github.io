DSP Basics

DSP Basics is FREE (previously available on our bores.com website since 1998 but now on our online training site) allows access as a guest. It introduces some basics of Digital Signal Processing including:

  • sampling, aliasing, reconstruction and quantization
  • correlation and convolution
  • frequency analysis and Fourier transforms
  • spectral resolution, leakage and windowing

The book takes a practical approach and does not assume a lot of prior knowledge.

This book has been incredibly popular for many years, and remains an excellent first step. To become a real practitioner in DSP you really should follow one of our more recent DSP online books

  • DSP Lite - £5 GBP
  • DSP Foundation - £15 GBP

These deal in more depth both with practical engineering questions and with some of the more difficult concepts and ideas of DSP that are often misunderstood.
