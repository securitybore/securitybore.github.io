Introduction to DSP eBook

The Introduction to DSP eBook encapsulates the information from our highly respected DSP course in a simple, low cost format that you can use for self learning. It gives a good introduction to the principles and practice of Digital Signal Processing and takes a practical, graphical approach, making it a particularly useful learning resource for engineers who will have to work with DSP rather than study it academically.

DSP eBook contents:

  • Sampled data systems
  • Antialiasing and reconstruction
  • Convolution and correlation
  • Spectral analysis and the Fourier Transform
  • Purpose and use of FFT window functions
  • Digital filtering
  • FIR and IIR filters
Our eBooks can be viewed on your computer and printed out as a useful book in full colour - to view and print the eBook all you need is the Adobe Acrobat Reader software which can be downloaded free of charge.
