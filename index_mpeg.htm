MPEG classes

MPEG video compression is a key component of most video-related products. Our MPEG classes teach you not only the conceptual and practical background that enable you to properly understand what MPEG does and why, but also the important and often neglected aspect of the MPEG bit-stream itself, how it is encoded and how to analyze one.
