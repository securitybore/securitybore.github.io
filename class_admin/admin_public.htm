Public classes

Public classes are held at our office in Woking, Surrey, UK. Woking is 25 minutes from Waterloo Station in London and convenient for Heathrow and Gatwick airports. There is bus from Heathrow to Woking every 30 mins. Our dedicated seminar room is very comfortable and there are facilities for hands-on exercies.

Classes are open to anyone. We keep classes sizes small (a maximum of 6) as we find this encourages interactions between Chris and the attendees: he likes lots of questions, espcially difficult ones that makes him stop and think! Classes are run even if we only have one attendee, so apart from accident/illness/death you can be sure the class will go ahead.

The charge is £200 per day. This includes free access to our online training site where there are extensive notes to accompany the training.
