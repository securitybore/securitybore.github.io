Onsite Training Classes

Our training classes can be given anywhere in the world and if you have 4-5 people to train, it is more economical in money and time to arrange an onsite class rather than attend a public class. There are many advantages to onsite training:

  • Saves time and money
  • Classes can be customised to your requirements
  • Key people do not have to be away from work, or home
  • Class can be arranged at a time to suit you
  • Class timings can be arranged to suit you
  • Delegates can attend only the parts of the class that is useful to them,
    though we would always recomend attending all sessions

Prices depend on location, number of delegates and length of the class. Further pricing information can be found on the Charges page.
