The standard-charge allows a set number of engineers to attend the class. This is the included-number-of-attendees. The standard-charge and the corresponding included-number-of-attendees varies depending on the location and the length of the class.

  • If fewer than the included-number-of-attendees attend the standard-charge is not reduced.
  • If more than the included-number-of-attendees attend then the additional attendees recieve a discount on the per-person charge.
  • The standard-charge varies depending on location, but as standard-charge increases the included-number-of-attendees also increases.
  • The standard-charge includes all costs: no additional charges are made for notes, hotel or travel.
