Onsite classes

Each onsite class has a standard-charge with a corresponding set number of attendees included in the price. We call this "set number" the included-number-of-attendees. The standard-charge and the corresponding included-number-of-attendees varies depending on the location and the length of the class. Consequently, the per person is the same worldwide. The per person charge ranges from £150 to £450 per day depending on the total number of attendees and the length of the class.

For different locations (UK, Europe, Asia, USA, other) we make a standard charge for an onsite class. For locations which involve a lot of time and expense to travel to the standard charge is higher, but more attendees are included, so the per person charge is the same as in a location where a lower standard charge is applicable.

  • If fewer than the included-number-of-attendees attend the standard-charge is not reduced.
  • If more than the included-number-of-attendees attend then the additional attendees recieve a 50% discount on the per-person charge.
  • The standard-charge varies depending on location, but as standard-charge increases the included-number-of-attendees also increases.
  • The standard-charge includes all costs: no additional charges are made for notes, hotel or travel.
